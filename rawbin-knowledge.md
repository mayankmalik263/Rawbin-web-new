## 2. Product: Rawbin — The Smart Home Composter

### Description
Rawbin is India's first smart home composter — an IoT-powered device (patent pending) that converts kitchen waste into organic fertilizer in just 7 days, completely automatically. 

### Key Value Propositions
1. **100% Odor-Free, Worm-Free, Fly-Free:** Advanced active carbon filtration system neutralizes organic odors completely. Perfect for indoor use. Only smells earthy.
2. **Apartment Friendly:** Compact, premium aesthetics. Can be kept in kitchens or balconies.
3. **Autonomy & Sensors:** Advanced sensor array monitors temperature, humidity, heat, and microclimate. Blinks LED when compost is ready.
4. **Energy Efficient:** Highly energy-efficient. Consumes less than 4 units of electricity per month (costs less than a standard LED bulb).
5. **Noise Level:** Whisper-quiet operation (operating under 35dB).
6. **Self-Cleaning:** Easy clean. Turn off power, spray water, stir, and rinse from the bottom.
7. **Continuous Adding:** Compost as you throw. Keep adding waste on top, harvest compost from the bottom drawer when ready.
8. **Closed-Loop System:** Uses the same compost to seed the next cycle (curd-milk model). No repeated inoculant purchases.

## 3. What Can Be Composted (Yes/No Guide)

### ✅ YES (Compostable)
- All organic food leftovers: uneaten salad, extra rice, leftover curry, rotten dough, bread, vegetables.
- Fruit scraps and peels (including citrus, banana, orange, and lemon peels).
- Eggshells, coffee grounds, tea leaves, expired cheese.
- Used non-synthetic cotton pads.

### ❌ NO (Do NOT add)
- Plastics, metals, glass, synthetic fibers.
- Chemical cleaners, paints, medicines.
- Large hard animal bones (e.g. beef/mutton bones), which can jam or stress the stirring blades.
- Large quantities of liquid cooking oil.
- Pet waste.

## 4. How Rawbin Works (3-Step Cycle)
1. **Put food waste:** Drop in organic waste (leftovers, peels, bread, rice).
2. **Add Rawbin mixture:** Add a scoop of Rawbin mixture (like adding detergent to a washing machine) to speed up composting.
3. **Press the top button:** Close the lid and press the button. The blades mix clockwise and counterclockwise.

## 6. Composting Science
- **Three-Phase Cycle:** Combines Mesophilic → Thermophilic → Mesophilic phases together automatically.
- **Smart Heat Saving:** Sensor-driven internal heating system. If in a warm environment, the heater scales down and uses natural environment heat to save power.
- **Oxygenation:** Mixing blades keep the mixture oxygenated, preventing anaerobic pocketing which causes smells.

## 9. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
- **What do I do with unused compost?** Contact us at `compostdrop@newcycl.com` for our compost drop program.
- **How to clean Rawbin?** Turn off power, spray inside with water, stir, and drain from the bottom.
- **How long does the active carbon filter last?** Between 3 to 6 months depending on usage, and is easily replaceable.
- **What to do with compost?** Mix it with garden or potted soil in a 1:4 or 1:5 ratio for plants.
